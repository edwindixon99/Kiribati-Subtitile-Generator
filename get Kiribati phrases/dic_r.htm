ra1. intj. suf. of exclamation or surprise.  ex. ai tamaroa ra!: how beautiful!  ai tena ra!: how tight! 
ra2. adj. suf. of interrogation: what? which?  ex. te aomata ra?: which man?  baika ra?: what things?  n te tabo ra?: in what place?  au ra, am ra, ana ra: my, thy, his, your, what?  tera?: what?  am ra te bai anne?: for what is that thing of yours?  rau? ram? rana?: what?
